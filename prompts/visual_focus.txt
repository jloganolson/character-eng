- ephemeral_questions: 0-2 intent-specific questions (what the character is actively looking for)
- constant_sam_targets: 1-2 always-tracked object types (usually includes "person")
- ephemeral_sam_targets: 0-2 intent-specific objects to detect

Respond with JSON:
{
  "constant_questions": ["How many people are near the stand?"],
  "ephemeral_questions": ["Is anyone holding money?"],
  "constant_sam_targets": ["person"],
